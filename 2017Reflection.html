My Story

Hello World! Decided to give blogging a shot. Welcome to my tiny tiny corner of the internet.My favourite question in the world is why. So I'll start by sharing why I'm doing all of this in the first place. First reason, I've always wanted to start a blog. Second Reason, I've always wanted to start a blog. I'm a strong believer that if there's something you long to do deep down, there's a reason for it and as long as we're alive we need to honor those aches in our soul.

Why "In A Frenzy"?
FRENZY [definition] A state or period of uncontrolled excitement or wild behaviour.
I was sitting at an airport, sleep deprived and thinking that everything in my life has just been happening at lighting speed. And then it hit me. It seems like we’re always in a frenzy to do something, to be something when we're young. We’re all yearning for something, searching in a frenzy, hurting in a frenzy, loving in a frenzy. And maybe that’s the thing about being young, we’re constantly in a frenzy, life is confusing and awesome and pretty much crazy. And when it’s not an insane roller coaster we make it dramatic, we need those ups and downs, anything to feel like we’re alive. I want to work in a frenzy, I want to create in a frenzy

So, where does this leave me now? I just want somewhere to express myself, that's it. That's why this blog exists. If anything I write or create resonates with you, thank you for letting me share my life with you and be part of your journey. :)
